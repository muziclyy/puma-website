About the Event

Vikshit Bharat 2025 is a flagship event organized at SMJN, dedicated to India's transformative journey across sectors. Our mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the next generation of innovators, entrepreneurs, and leaders.

  • Vision: To foster a culture of innovation, self-reliance, and national pride by bringing together India's brightest minds.
  • What to Expect? Interactive exhibitions, live tech demos, startup pitches, defence showcases, and expert panels.
  • Who Should Attend? Students, entrepreneurs, investors, defence enthusiasts, tech lovers, and anyone passionate about India's growth!
